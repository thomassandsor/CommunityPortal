Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 20, 2025

Data Collection

When you use our Community Portal, we collect:

  • Email Address: Used as your unique identifier
  • Name: First and last name for contact records
  • Mobile Phone: Optional contact information
  • Authentication Data: Managed by Clerk.dev

Data Storage

Your contact information is stored securely in Microsoft Dataverse using industry-standard security practices:

  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access is controlled via Service Principal authentication
  • Email addresses are used as unique identifiers and cannot be changed

Data Usage

We use your data solely for:

  • Creating and managing your contact record
  • Providing access to the Community Portal
  • Demonstrating React + Dataverse integration patterns

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Clerk.dev: For authentication services
  • Microsoft: For data storage in Dataverse
  • Netlify: For hosting and function execution

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your contact data
  • Update your contact information
  • Request deletion of your data (contact us)
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Security

We implement multiple security measures:

  • Input validation and sanitization
  • Rate limiting on API requests
  • Secure HTTP headers
  • Token caching with proper expiration
  • No client-side storage of sensitive data

Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please contact us through GitHub issues in the Community Portal repository.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
